# Quest Y-2 - The 30-day 2-minute challenge

> **Win condition:** a thirty-row log shows real two-minute AI-assisted work across the month, plus a short reflection on what changed in your week.

This quest is about habit, not heroics. Two minutes a day beats one dramatic sprint that never repeats.

---

## Prerequisite

Quest Y-1 should be complete or in flight. You need a place to record the log: a tracker row, note, doc, or approved cohort surface.

The log should be light enough that logging never becomes the work.

---

## The task

For thirty workdays, do one tiny AI-assisted action and record it.

Good two-minute tasks:

- ask Claude to explain a diff before review;
- turn rough notes into a clean update;
- summarize a thread into decisions and open questions;
- ask for three possible causes of a small bug;
- improve a PR title;
- draft a test plan;
- ask "what context is missing before I act?"

Bad two-minute tasks:

- open Claude Code and quit;
- paste sensitive material;
- ask a vague question with no work consequence;
- retroactively fill a month of logs;
- count passive reading as AI-assisted work.

---

## Log template

```markdown
| Date | Two-minute task | Surface used | What surprised me | Follow-up |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD |  |  |  |  |
```

Keep entries short.

Example:

```markdown
| 2026-04-27 | Asked Claude to separate evidence from inference on a bug triage | Claude Code | One likely file was pure inference | Re-ran search with narrower scope |
```

---

## Weekly check-in

Once a week, write three bullets:

```markdown
## Week N

- One AI habit that helped:
- One thing I overused:
- One thing I will change next week:
```

The weekly note turns a streak into learning.

---

## Evidence template

```markdown
## Quest Y-2 evidence

Builder handle:
Start date:
End date:
Log link or screenshot:
Completed rows:
Weekly check-ins completed:

What changed in my week:
Reviewer or cohort lead:
Follow-up needed:
```

The "what changed" paragraph should be honest. It can say the habit helped less than expected if that is true.

---

## What counts

This counts:

- thirty real entries;
- each entry names a task and surface;
- at least four weekly check-ins for a full month;
- a final reflection;
- no sensitive data in the log.

This does not count:

- backfilled logs;
- screenshots with private details;
- pure reading without AI-assisted action;
- a leaderboard entry with no evidence;
- a habit tracker that says only "done."

---

## Common failure modes

**"I missed a day."** Continue. The goal is practice, not shame. Mark the gap honestly.

**"The log got too detailed."** Reduce it. One line per day is enough.

**"Every entry is the same."** Add variety: explain, summarize, compare, draft, review, triage.

**"I used AI for work but forgot to log it."** Log immediately after the two-minute task.

---

## Success criteria

You pass Quest Y-2 when:

- the log has thirty honest entries;
- weekly check-ins exist;
- final reflection exists;
- the log contains no sensitive details;
- the practice clearly connects to real work.
